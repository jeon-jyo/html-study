The World's Ocean

The Atlantic Ocean is situated between the Americas and
European/African continents. Atlantic Ocean is the second largest and
saltiest ocean in the world. It resembles an S-shape between the
Americas, Europe and Africa. “Atlantic” originated from the Greek god
“Atlas” who carried the sky for eternity.
